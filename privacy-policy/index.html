By using MyLandIQ, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our App.

3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name (optional), when you create an account
  • Property Data: Addresses and land areas you enter for analysis
  • Payment Information: Transaction details processed through Google Play (we do not store card numbers)

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, analysis history, app interactions
  • Log Data: Error reports, crash logs for improving app stability

3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise GPS location (we only use the address you enter)
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Sensitive personal data (health, biometrics, caste, religion)

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We process your personal data for the following lawful purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide AI-powered property analysis and recommendations
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account
  • Payment Processing: To process credit purchases and maintain transaction records
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Communication: To send service-related notifications and respond to inquiries
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, including GST requirements

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when creating an account and using our services
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing necessary to provide the services you requested
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required to comply with Indian laws
  • Legitimate Interest: For service improvement and security purposes

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service Providers:
    • Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics)
    • Anthropic (Claude AI API for property analysis - only property addresses, not personal data)
    • Google Play (payment processing)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or government request
Important: We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored on Google Firebase servers with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data transmission
  • Regular security assessments and updates are performed

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Analysis History: Retained for 2 years or until account deletion
  • Payment Records: Retained for 8 years as required by GST regulations
  • Deletion Logs: Retained for 30 days for security purposes

8. Your Rights Under DPDP Act

As a Data Principal, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another person to exercise your rights

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  • In-App: Go to Account → Settings → Delete Account (for erasure)
  • Email: Contact privacy@mylandiq.com with your request

We will respond to your request within 30 days.
