3. Technical Failures & Credit Restoration

Good News: If an analysis fails due to technical issues on our end, your credit will be automatically restored to your account.

3.1 Automatic Credit Restoration

Credits are automatically restored when:

  • Server errors prevent analysis completion
  • AI service (Claude API) timeout or failure
  • Network issues on our infrastructure
  • App crashes during analysis processing

3.2 Manual Credit Restoration

If your credit wasn't automatically restored after a failed analysis:

  1. Note the date, time, and property address of the failed analysis
  2. Email support@mylandiq.com with details
  3. We'll verify and restore your credit within 24-48 hours

Note: Credits are NOT restored for user errors such as entering invalid addresses, incomplete information, or cancelling analysis mid-process.

4. How to Request a Refund

4.1 Option A: Through Google Play (Recommended)

  1. Open Google Play Store on your device
  2. Tap your profile icon ‚Üí Payments & subscriptions
  3. Tap Budget & history
  4. Find the MyLandIQ purchase ‚Üí Tap Refund
  5. Follow the prompts to complete your request

Google typically processes refunds within 1-4 business days.

4.2 Option B: Contact Us Directly

Email billing@mylandiq.com with:

  • Subject: "Refund Request - [Your Email]"
  • Google Play order number (from receipt email)
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund
  • Number of credits used (if any)

Response Time: We respond to refund requests within 2-3 business days. Approved refunds are processed through Google Play within 5-10 business days.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided for:

  • Credits that have been fully used
  • Purchases made more than 7 days ago
  • Dissatisfaction with AI analysis results (analysis is informational only)
  • Changes in personal circumstances or investment plans
  • Account termination due to Terms of Service violations
  • Inability to use the app due to device incompatibility
