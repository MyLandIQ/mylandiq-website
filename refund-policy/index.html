Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Our Commitment: We want you to be satisfied with MyLandIQ. If you're not happy with your purchase, we're here to help with a fair and transparent refund process.

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all purchases made through the MyLandIQ mobile application ("App"). All payments are processed through Google Play, and this policy operates in conjunction with Google Play's refund policies.

We offer refunds under specific circumstances as outlined below. Our goal is to be fair and reasonable while preventing abuse of the system.

2. Credit Purchases

MyLandIQ uses a credit-based system where you purchase credits to use for property analysis:

Package Price (incl. GST) Credits
Single Analysis ₹99 1 Credit
Value Pack ₹399 5 Credits
Professional Pack ₹999 15 Credits

3. Refund Eligibility

3.1 Eligibility Matrix

Scenario Refund Eligibility Time Limit
Unused credits (no analysis performed) ✓ Eligible Within 7 days
Partially used credits ◐ Pro-rated Within 7 days
All credits used ✗ Not Eligible —
Technical failure (analysis didn't complete) ✓ Credit Restored Any time
Duplicate purchase (accidental) ✓ Full Refund Within 48 hours
Unauthorized purchase ◐ Case by Case Report immediately
Dissatisfied with analysis quality ◐ Credit/Partial Within 48 hours

3.2 Full Refund Scenarios

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You purchased credits but have not used any of them
  • The refund request is made within 7 days of purchase
  • You made a duplicate purchase by mistake
  • The purchase was unauthorized (and you can provide evidence)

3.3 Pro-rated Refund

If you have used some but not all of your credits within 7 days of purchase:

  • We will refund the value of unused credits
  • Example: Purchased 5 credits (₹399), used 2 → Refund for 3 unused credits (₹239)

3.4 Credit Restoration (No Cash Refund)

Your credits will be restored (not cash refund) in these cases:

  • Technical failure prevented analysis completion
  • App crashed during analysis and result wasn't delivered
  • Server error resulted in incomplete or missing analysis

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Note: The following situations do NOT qualify for refunds:
  • Used Credits: Credits that have been consumed for completed analyses
  • After 7 Days: Requests made more than 7 days after purchase (for unused credits)
  • Changed Mind: Simply deciding you don't want to use the service (after using credits)
  • Investment Decisions: Losses or unfavorable outcomes from decisions based on our analysis
  • Third-Party Issues: Problems caused by your device, internet, or Google Play
  • Account Termination: Credits remaining when account is terminated for policy violations

5. How to Request a Refund

5.1 Through Google Play (Recommended)

For the fastest processing, request a refund through Google Play:

  1. Open Google Play Store on your device
  2. Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Budget & history
  3. Find the MyLandIQ purchase
  4. Tap "Request a refund" and follow the prompts

5.2 Direct Request to MyLandIQ

You can also contact us directly:

  1. Email support@mylandiq.com
  2. Include your registered email address
  3. Provide Google Play order ID (found in your purchase receipt)
  4. State the reason for your refund request
  5. Include any relevant screenshots or details

6. Refund Processing

6.1 Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 24 hours of receiving your request
  • Decision: Within 3 business days
  • Processing: 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account

6.2 Refund Method

Refunds are processed through the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card → Refund to card (5-10 business days)
  • UPI → Refund to UPI-linked account (3-5 business days)
  • Net Banking → Refund to bank account (5-10 business days)
  • Google Play Balance → Refund to Play Balance (1-3 business days)

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Quality Concerns

If you believe the analysis quality was significantly below expectations:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of the analysis
  • Explain the specific issues with the analysis
  • We may offer credit restoration or partial refund on a case-by-case basis
Note: Our AI analysis is for informational purposes. Disagreement with recommendations alone doesn't qualify for a refund, but technical errors or obviously incorrect information may qualify.

7.2 Unauthorized Purchases

If someone made a purchase without your authorization:

  • Report immediately to both us and Google Play
  • Provide evidence that the purchase was unauthorized
  • Consider enabling purchase authentication on your device

8. GST and Tax Implications

All prices include GST (Goods and Services Tax) at 18%. For refunds:

  • The full amount including GST will be refunded
  • GST adjustments will be handled in our accounting
  • You don't need to take any action regarding taxes
