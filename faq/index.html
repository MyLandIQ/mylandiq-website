What is MyLandIQ?
MyLandIQ is an AI-powered mobile app that analyzes land and property to provide development recommendations, market valuations, and investment insights. Simply enter a property address and land size, and our AI generates detailed analysis including best use cases, estimated costs, revenues, and ROI projections.
How do I create an account?
Download the app from Google Play Store and sign in with your Google account. It's quick and secure - no separate password needed!
Is MyLandIQ available on iOS?
Currently, MyLandIQ is available only on Android devices through the Google Play Store. We're working on an iOS version and will announce when it's available.
Which countries are supported?
MyLandIQ works best for properties in India, with optimized analysis for cities across all tiers (metros, large cities, and small towns). We also support properties in USA, UK, UAE, Australia, Canada, and other English-speaking markets with region-specific pricing data.

üìä Analysis & Results

How accurate is the analysis?
Our AI provides estimates based on location, property size, and market data. While we strive for accuracy, results are for informational purposes only and should not replace professional advice from real estate consultants, architects, or financial advisors. Market conditions vary and actual results may differ.
What information do I need to provide?
For the best analysis, provide:
  • Property Address: As specific as possible (street, area, city, state)
  • Land Area: Total plot size
  • Unit: Square feet, square yards, square meters, marla, kanal, or acres
How long does analysis take?
Analysis typically completes within 15-30 seconds. During high demand periods, it may take up to 60 seconds. If analysis fails due to technical issues, your credit will be automatically restored.
Can I save and share my analysis?
Yes! All your analyses are saved to your account history. You can view past analyses anytime and export them as PDF reports to share with others.
